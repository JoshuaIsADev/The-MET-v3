European Paintings

Known for landscapes and imaginary city views, Guardi also painted a few interiors including this image depicting Venetian officials and petitioners outside the principal council chamber of the Doge’s palace. Although Guardi has telescoped his view in order to accommodate the scene, the room is otherwise accurately depicted. Guardi’s pendant depicting the Ridotto Pubblico is also in The Met’s collection.

On view at The Met Fifth Avenue in Gallery 541
